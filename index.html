Shimpei Makimoto (牧本 慎平) is a software engineer, a member of www.ruby-lang.org site editorial team, and working for Cookpad Inc. He received an M.E. in Information Science from Nara Institute of Science and Technology in 2008. His recent interests are in mobile application development, English pronouns, and daily cooking. He loves coffee and glitch arts.
